# Academic Projects

These projects represent coursework and academic competitions I've completed at Texas A&M. They demonstrate my skills in engineering design, team collaboration, and applied problem-solving in structured environments.

## Projects

### [Robotic Arm Control System](/projects/academic-projects/robotic-arm-control-system/)
A control system for a 4-DOF robotic arm using inverse kinematics and real-time motor feedback.

### [MATLAB Water Filtration Simulation](/projects/academic-projects/water-filtration-simulation/)
A simulation of membrane filtration dynamics using MATLAB to visualize pressure drops and contaminant separation.

### [Aggies Invent: Pipe Filter for Carbon Capture](/projects/academic-projects/carbon-pipe-filter/)
A 48-hour rapid prototyping competition where my team designed and pitched a modular filter for industrial carbon capture.
