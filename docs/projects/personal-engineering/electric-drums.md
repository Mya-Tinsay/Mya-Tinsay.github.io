# Converting an Electronic Drum Set

**Date:** Summer 2025
**Tools:** Soldering Iron, 

Converting an old Guitar Hero drum set into a MIDI-compatible electronic drum set. 

---

## Problem and Purpose

While helping my cousin clear out space for his 3D printing business, I came across his old, broken Wii Guitar Hero drum set, which I'd grown up playing. Around the same time, I had been exploring drum covers and digital drumming content, which made me realize there was potential to repurpose the set rather than discard it.

After researching, I found that many hobbyists convert gaming drum kits into MIDI-compatible electronic drums. My goal for this project is to transform the Guitar Hero drum set into a functional MIDI input device that connects directly to my laptop. I want to preserve its familiar form factor while enabling it to trigger accurate drum sounds for practice and music creation.
